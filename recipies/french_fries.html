The best second best food in the world

French fries are a popular side dish and snack consisting of potatoes cut into strips and deep-fried until golden brown and crispy, with a soft, fluffy interior and a crispy crust. They are typically seasoned with salt and served with condiments such as ketchup or mayonnaise, and come in various forms like curly, shoestring, or steak fries.

Some of the most important ingredients needed for it are :

  • cooking oil
