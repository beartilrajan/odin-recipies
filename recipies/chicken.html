Chicken Tasty Recipie

The best food in the world

Chicken curry is a flavorful, spiced chicken dish stewed in a rich sauce, with ingredients like onions, ginger, garlic, tomatoes, and a blend of aromatic spices such as cumin, coriander, and turmeric forming its complex base.

Some of the most important ingredients needed for it are :

  • chicken pieces (breast, thighs)
  • onions
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • Spices

Steps to create the most tasty chicken curry :

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and saute chopped onions until golden
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook briefly
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and spices, cook into a thick masala
  4. Add chicken pieces and stir well
  5. Cover and cook until chicken are tender
  6. FInish with garam masala and fresh coriander leaves
